Hi, I'm Hemanth Sabbella, a first year PhD student at SCIS, Singapore Management University (SMU) advised by Prof. Archan Misra and Prof. Dong Ma. Currently a visiting student at M3S, under MIT's SMART Program

I graduated from IIIT-Delhi with a degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering. Previously, I worked as a Senior Project Associate at Indian Institute of Science (IISc) under Prof. Chetan Singh Thakur, followed by Research Engineer at SMU.

I specialize in deep learning for MCUs (tinyML), low-power embedded systems, digital and analog circuits. My current research broadly encompasses neuromorphic computing (SNNs), low-power wearable sensing systems, and resource-constrained models, exploring their integration into ubiquitous and robotic applications.

In addition to my professional pursuits, I have a passion for photography (Featured in natgeo, you can check out my work @foto.obscura), enjoy reading non-fiction, and have a diverse palette when it comes to food.
