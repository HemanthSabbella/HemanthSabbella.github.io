Ultrasound imaging

Dhvani

A low-cost, portable, super-resolution ultrasound imaging system using an 8-channel transceiver and 16 analog multiplexers to fold 128-channel probe hardware by 16×, reducing cost. Contributions included hardware development, testing, and software scripts.
